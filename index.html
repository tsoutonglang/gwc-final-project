Willy the Whale

Welcome to the Game!

Welcome to Willy's World Adventure! When Willy's home is contaminated by human pollution, he is forced to relocate. You have to help him navigate the polluted waters. Collect three stars for Willy to sucsessfully arrive at his new home with a gift for his friend who is waiting for him there.

INSTRUCTIONS:
click the green flag to start
once the video ends, press the spacebar to start playing
use the arrow keys to move Willy up, down, left, and right
collect three starfish to finish the game
don't hit the trash three times or Willy will be too hurt to continue
click the stop sign to end the game

songs used: House of Cards instrumental - BTS
Butterfly instrumental - BTS

A few million years ago the world was flourishing and teeming with life. Plants were lush, animals were healthy, and the world was at peace. With human activity and modern advancements, planet Earth has slowly been decaying more and more over the years. Animals are especially impacted by our actions and have been dying or unable to survive in their natural habitats. A particularly important area are the sea or water creatures that are impacted by humans. Thousands of people are constantly throwing trash into waters and harming the animals. Pollution also contaminates the waters and causes animals to become ill with disease. These animals are dying at an alarming rate and becoming more rare.

It has been brought to our attention that the situation is only getting worse and it is our responsibility as humans to fix the problem we started. We hope that through our interactive game and resourceful website people will become more aware and desire to help the animals.
